It's not about your coding skills.
It's more about handling your own mind when racing.

The best and primary advice is to <u>be calm</u>.

## What's tested

It's not about your skills of writing code.
It's about your <u>skills of thinking</u> clearly.

You're not being tested for perfection.
You're being tested for clarity.
## Practice out loud

Vocalize your thinking process.
Explain as if you'd do to a trainee.

## High-lever picture

Understand what the program should solve.

## Clarify - Plan - Code - Test - Optimize

Most important are:
- Clarify <u>all the edge cases</u>.
- Plan how to implement.

## Prepare for the interview

Prepare a <u>Google sheets</u> page.
Prepare a <u>lucid diagram</u> or other tool for diagrams.
Air the room.
Don't eat sugar.
