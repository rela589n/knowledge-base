**Distillation [[Design Documents and Diagrams|Document]]** - contains most **essentials objects** and **relationships**, usually represented as [[Diagram]].

It gives a **broad view** of the [[Domain Model|Model]], 
***focusing*** *on* ***how*** main **parts *fit*** *together*.

To be understood by **non-technical** people.

It ***indicates*** the **importance** of a change.

**Changes** to the doc ***originate*** from:
- ***changes*** *to* the **[[Core Domain|Core]]**;
- *or* ***changes*** *to* its **boundaries**.

Both **require**:
- **consultation** to other team members;
- **notification** of all.
