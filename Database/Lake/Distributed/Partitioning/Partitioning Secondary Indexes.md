**[[Secondary Index|Secondary Indexes]] do not uniquely identify** records.

[[Partitioning]] becomes **complicated** really fast, as we don't know neither **which [[Partition]] to look in** nor where the record is located within the [[Partition]].

![[Full Text Search Engine#^1f7e6d]]

Approaches (schemes) to **partition database with secondary indexes**:
- **[[Document-based Secondary Indexes Partitioning|Document-Based]]** partitioning;
- **[[Term-based Secondary Indexes Partitioning|Term-Based]]** partitioning.
