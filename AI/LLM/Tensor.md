**Tensor** is an abstraction over number for dimensions:
- 0-dimensional (rank) tensor: just a scalar: `0.23`
- 1-dimensional (rank) tensor: array: `[1.32, 0.234]`

For example, `tensor<int8>`.

**Indexed:** (indexed array) - dense
**Mapped:** (string map) - sparse (named)
